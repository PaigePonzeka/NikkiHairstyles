Hey ya'll, I'm Nikki Lee!

I began my career as a hairstylist in 2007 within the theatre industry. After years of private school education, salon experiences, and on site bridal work, I decided that I wanted to create and build my own business, Ink & Ivy. I established myself as a seasoned hair stylist and now I'm known as a boho hair expert in the bridal industry. I not only own my own business, but I have a team of ladies that work along side me at over 150 weddings a year and you can find me at my studio located in Port Jefferson Station, NY.
